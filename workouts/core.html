## Core The idea of this workout is to train the core as a whole using various exercises at a high intensity. ## 3 Sets\n ### Insert planks in between each core exercise\n ### Change exercise every 30 secs.\n - Butterfly Crunches\n - Spider Plank\n - Flutter Kick - Russian Twist - Mountain Climber Therefore you would be doing butterfly crunches, followed by planks,followed by spider planks and then planks again and so on.
